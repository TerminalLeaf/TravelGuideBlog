A Weekend on the Seine

I was lucky enough to spend three days visiting Paris this month! I went with a friend and we were able to see many things while we were there!

Click here to learn more about paris!

Day 1

On our first day, we started by visiting Montmarte right away. Our second stop was the Eiffel Tower (check out that beautiful picture!) We ate dinner at a cute little bistro overlooking la Seine.

Day 2

On our second day, we went shopping on Avenue des Champs Elysees. Every single person we saw was dressed in the height of fashion.

After we finished shopping, we visited some really beautiful places! We went to Notre Dame. We went off on a maracon tour of Paris and ate all the best macarons the city had to offer. After we finished shopping, we visited le Louvre.
